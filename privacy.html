Privacy Policy

How Jeme Group Inc handles information.

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Overview

Jeme Group Inc respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or contact us about advertising services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information you provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, business name, and message details.
  • Basic website usage information, such as browser type, pages viewed, and general traffic patterns, if analytics tools are used.
  • Information included in emails, calls, forms, or other communications you choose to send to us.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and schedule consultations.
  • To understand advertising needs and prepare relevant recommendations.
  • To improve our website, services, communication, and customer experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our business, clients, and website.

Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate our website, communicate with clients, or deliver advertising services. We may also disclose information when required by law or when necessary to protect our rights.

SMS and Mobile Information

If you provide a phone number and opt in to receive text messages, we use that number to send messages related to Jeme Group Inc advertising services, consultations, and customer support. Mobile opt-in information and text messaging consent will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to support basic site functionality and understand website performance. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, but no website or electronic communication is completely secure. Please avoid sending sensitive personal, financial, or confidential information through general contact forms or email unless necessary.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request updates, corrections, or deletion of information you have provided, subject to any legal or business recordkeeping requirements. You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP, or request help by replying HELP.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Jeme Group Inc at admin@jemegroupinc.com or 347-221-6055.
